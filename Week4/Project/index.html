About

Cafe Sospeso (also known as suspended coffee) is a coffee house that thrives on the idea of charity. Suspended coffee/pending coffee is when a cup of coffee has already been paid for in advanced through the act of charity. This act of kindness began in Naples, Italy when someone who experienced good luck would order a suspeso, they would pay for 2 coffees but would receive 1. Later when a poor person enquired if there was a sospeso available they would receive the free coffee

What's your favorite Cafe Shop?

Our coffee may be stronger then our competitors but with our organic beans and special roast handling we are able to cut out the strong bitterness so you taste nothing but pure silk, smooth and rich flavors.
